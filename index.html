Inhabits coastal shore and reef slope rubble areas experiencing good clear and turbid water movement. Grows similar to Colt Coral, however, has a dry feel to its tissue, not the slimy feel associated with Colt Coral. Can reach heights of about 18 inches (45 cm).

Heliopore coerulea can resist different environments, so they are often displayed in tropical aquariums. Southeast Asia and Italy will extract a syrup from it and drink it in pearl milk tea.

The main characteristics of separating members of different genera are distinguished by the degree and type of acupuncture. Other judgment features include branching pattern and whole adult shape.

Isis hippuris is a species of bamboo coral found in the Western Pacific Ocean. It produces hippuristanol, a molecule with apparent anti-cancer functions.

Pinnigorgia flava is a fast growing gorgonian coral that is a graceful, thin-branched that can be described as tan to pink in color with similarly colored polyps.

Sinularia brassica are a unique soft coral. They are some of sought after and the most attractive corals found in the world's oceans today.
